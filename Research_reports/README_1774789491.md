Smart Helmet AI for real-time safety compliance on construction sites

## Problem Statement
Construction sites face significant challenges in maintaining safety standards due to dynamic risks, limited visibility, and high human error rates. Traditional safety monitoring systems often lag behind real-time threats, leaving workers exposed to hazards.

## Solution (Resilience Triad Scheme: Executor, Overseer, Arbiter)
The proposed project introduces a cutting-edge Smart Helmet AI that works through a resilience triad. The Executor continuously executes safety protocols, the Overseer monitors compliance across teams, and the Arbiter makes rapid decisions to mitigate risks. This coordinated approach ensures proactive safety management.

## Unique Proposition (Digital Stress-Test)
What sets this solution apart is its advanced digital stress-testing capabilities. By simulating extreme scenarios in real time, the system evolves and strengthens its protective mechanisms, offering unprecedented reliability under pressure.

## Economic Benefits
Implementing Smart Helmet AI results in significant cost savings by reducing accidents, minimizing downtime, and lowering insurance premiums. The long-term economic gain comes from a safer workforce and optimized operational efficiency.

This project redefines safety on construction sites through intelligent innovation.
